Meet Dr. Adam

Kirkpatrick

Member of the American Dental Association, the Oregon Dental Association, the Southern Willamette Dental Society, the Oregon Coalition of Minimally Invasive Dentistry, and the American Academy of Cosmetic Dentistry.

Dr. Kirkpatrick was born and raised right here in Linn County. He graduated from Oregon State University (Go, Beavers!) before earning his doctorate from the prestigious University of the Pacific in San Francisco. Each year he continues to research the latest advances in cosmetic and family dentistry with the nation's foremost clinicians, gaining him membership into the World Congress of Minimally Invasive Dentistry. Dr. Kirkpatrick's use of the latest advances in technology - including invisible orthodontics, digital x-rays, one visit crowns, dental implants and CariFree cavity detection - will help you and your entire family maintain beautiful, healthy smiles for a lifetime.

Dr. Kirkpatrick and his wife, Kelsie have 3 children - Dailey, Arianna and Kellen. When he's not practicing dentistry, Dr. Kirkpatrick can be found enjoying the outdoors with his family or taking in an OSU sporting event (Go Beavers!)
